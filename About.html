About Bilion

How Did Bilion Start?

Bilion was started by a 14 year old kid named Derek Haber, because he had an idea to start a new search engine. He worked on it privately on it keeping it offline. This website was began in April, 2019, and was launched 5/7/2019. He also requested a friend to make the logo. The logo went through many stages, from plain silver to the new 3-Dimensional logo we have now. If you have any ideas to make our products more supportive and immersive for our users, make sure to Contact me and send me your ideas.
